Introduction

SUN is a project that aims to help people with a pathology to meet people who have lived or are living the same story.

Hello

SUN is a project that brings together two supports. An application and a media.

The launch

SUN started on 1 July 2019 with the launch of its networks. In October, we launched a pool on KissKiss BankBank to finance our project.

Hello

Within a month, we managed to complete the pool and launch the development of the application.

Hello

At the same time, the media has gained momentum with the videos and the #JENPARLE movement, which aims to free speech around health taboos.

Our project

The goal of SUN is to succeed in removing the taboos around health, and to allow all people suffering from a pathology, as well as their loved ones, to free their word and help them to get better.

Hello

Our community is growing every day with more and more people affected by what we do.

Hello

The application and the media are simple tools made available to help everyone to accept themselves and to show that they are not alone.
